Magdalena Le Blanc is a brand that seeks integration of crafts and traditions of Chilean native people with contemporary textile design. The project comes with a strong social sense, with the intention of providing employment for weavers and spinners in southern Chile, rescuing the textile art from their ancestors through design and manufacture of high quality. With a strong commitment to the environment and the artisans, we create unique pieces that give identity to spaces.
